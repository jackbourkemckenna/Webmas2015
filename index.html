Webmas 2015

For Great causes, 24 hours 17th December

What is Webmas?

Great & innovative sites built for Charities and Good causes; designed to support and enhance existing campaigns.

  • How? - On the 17th December, teams of students from NCI will spend just 24 hours designing, developing and deploying sites. Each team will be assigned an industry expert as a mentor for the day.
  • Why? - Students get real, hands on experience, charities get well designed, future proof websites. What's not to like?
